Reflection

Choosing Education as my course may be one of the best decisions I ever made. Although I cried at first because never in my life have I imagined to be a teacher. But as time goes by, I realized that being a teacher may not be bad after all, we get to see the sides of ourselves we never knew existed. I Iearned to become creative, innovative, and most importantly, I have learned to study harder. Being a teacher is an exhilarating feeling for me. Not only did I discover my full potential, but my students' as well.

Teaching is the art of imparting knowledge and shaping minds, is a noble calling that transcends boundaries and enriches lives. It is a transformative art that requires dedication, passion, and a profound understanding of human nature. Teachers are the catalysts who ignite the spark of curiosity, guide the path towards discovery, and foster a lifelong learning.

Teaching is a responsibility that we must be fully aware. We are the role models of the future generations. As a teacher, we must provide a safe space for students to express themselves freely and are not afraid to speak what's in their minds. We have to create a safe haven for our students ho feel more safe and create a harmonious relationship to one another.

As an aspiring teacher, I will do my best do impart knwoledge and values to my students. I will lead them achieving a better versions of themselves. It is my job to teach them the importance of education in life. Seeing my students' success is a great reward nothing can compare.
